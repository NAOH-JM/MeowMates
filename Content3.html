Understanding Cat Constipation

Symptoms

Cats suffering from constipation may show symptoms such as infrequent or absent bowel movements, straining in the litter box, hard or dry stools, and signs of discomfort or pain.

Causes

Constipation in cats can arise from various factors, including dehydration, ingestion of hair or foreign objects, dietary issues, or underlying medical conditions like obesity or neurological disorders.

Treatments

Treatment options include ensuring adequate hydration, adjusting the diet to include fiber, or seeking veterinary care for medication or other interventions.

Prevention

Preventive measures involve regular grooming, maintaining a balanced diet, and encouraging physical activity to support healthy digestion.
